About

PhilosophyOfShakespeare is a series of essays by Richard Burrow exploring Shakespeare's philosophy through close reading of his plays.

Richard is a retired English Literature teacher. He has taught secondary school and university students, and holds an MPhil from Oxford University.

PhilosophyOfShakespeare is a work in progress. New essays will be added regularly. Hopefully, each play will eventually have its own essay in this collection.

If you have any questions or comments, please get in touch using the email address philosophyofshakespeare@gmail.com.
